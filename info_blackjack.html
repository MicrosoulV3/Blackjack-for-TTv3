How to play Backjack

Blackjack is a very popular casino game, almost as popular as poker. Here are the rules of the game of blackjack.
Just collect cards and be the closest to 21 points.
The ace is worth 1 or 11 points, depending on your choice. The 10, jack, queen, and king are 10 points.
The other cards have their corresponding values, 5=5 points....
You can decide at any time that you want to keep your hand. If you exceed 21, you automatically lose.
If you make 21 you have a BlackJack.
The dealer has the advantage when there is a tie, so if both players have 20, the dealer wins.
The game is between each player and the dealer.


How to win at blackjack?

If the player exceeds 21 (busted) he must re-shuffle his first card and loses his bet.
If a player has Black Jack (aces and 10, or 21 with 2 cards) he also exposes his game immediately.
  
If the player is busted, blackjacked, or guarding his game,
the dealer passes to the next player... until his own turn.
The dealer turns over his first card and tries to beat the highest score.
He exposes his cards, unlike the others, because they will never play again.
When the dealer has finished playing, it is time to show his cards.
The remaining players, show their cards one by one, and compare it to the dealer.
Those who beat the dealer win, the others lose their bets to the dealer.
If there is a tie between a player and the dealer, the dealer always wins the hand.
